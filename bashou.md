## Matsuo Bashou (1644-1694)

Matsuo Bashou, born Matsuo Kinsaku, was the most famous poet 
in the Edo period in Japan.  He wrote thousands of haiku, such as:

* Shizukesa ya
* Iwa ni shimiiru
* Semi no koe

This translates roughly as:

* Tranquility
* Penetrating every rock
* A cicada's voice
